About Us

Our Mission and Products

At ModaNexus, our mission is to provide renewable and sustainable energy solutions for both commercial and domestic use. We offer a wide range of products, including solar panels, batteries, inverters, and lighting solutions. Our products are designed to be both aesthetically pleasing and functional, and we use the latest technology and equipment to ensure that they are of the highest quality.

ModaNexus has experienced significant growth over the past few years, expanding from 32 employees worldwide in 2016/2017 to 328 employees in 2022/2023. With 68 employees based in Europe, 68 in the Americas, 54 in Africa, 72 in Asia, and 66 in Oceania, our team is well-equipped to deliver renewable energy solutions on a global scale. Despite our growth, we remain committed to providing tailored solutions that meet our customers' unique needs, with a focus on sustainability, innovation, and customer satisfaction. As we continue to expand, we aim to grow to 1,000 employees worldwide over the next two years.

We differentiate ourselves from our competitors through our focus on low carbon and renewable technology, high-efficiency solutions, and commitment to sustainable growth. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring that our products and services are of the highest quality, from the initial consultation and design phase to the installation and maintenance of our products. We believe that sustainable energy solutions are the future, and we are committed to delivering products and services that help our customers make the transition to renewable energy.

Our Values and Commitment

We are a team of professionals committed to providing renewable energy solutions that make a positive impact on the environment. We believe that by reducing our reliance on fossil fuels and transitioning to renewable energy, we can create a more sustainable future for generations to come. Our team is dedicated to staying at the forefront of the renewable energy industry, investing in the latest technology and equipment to deliver high-quality and efficient solutions.

We are also committed to building long-lasting relationships with our customers, providing tailored solutions that meet their unique needs. Our core values of sustainability, innovation, and customer satisfaction guide everything we do. We believe that our commitment to these values sets us apart from our competitors and allows us to deliver products and services that exceed our customers' expectations. We are passionate about renewable energy and are committed to making a positive impact on the environment, one solution at a time.
