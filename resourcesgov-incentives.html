Government Incentives

Federal Solar Tax Credit

Homeowners can deduct up to 26% of the cost of their solar panel system from their federal taxes.

Learn More

State Tax Credits and Rebates

Many states offer their own tax credits or rebates for installing solar panels. Check with your state's energy office to see what incentives are available in your area.

Learn More

Utility Net Metering Programs

Homeowners can earn credits on their energy bills for excess energy their solar panels generate and feed back into the grid.

Learn More

Business Energy Investment Tax Credit

Businesses can deduct up to 26% of the cost of their solar panel system from their federal taxes.

Learn More

State Tax Credits and Rebates for Businesses

Many states offer their own tax credits or rebates for installing solar panels on commercial properties. Check with your state's energy office to see what incentives are available in your area.

Learn More

Utility Incentives for Businesses

Some utility companies offer special rates or incentives for businesses that install solar panels, such as lower energy rates or cash rebates.

Learn More

Make sure to consult with a qualified tax professional or solar energy expert to take full advantage of the incentives available to you.
